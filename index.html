Matthew's Interview with Jeremiah Tatum

Hello! My name is Matthew Wilson! A student at Base Camp Coding Academy, a non-profit that trains high school graduates to be full-stack developers. I interveiwed my fellow classmate, Jeremiah Tatum on November 19, 2020. Here is how it went.



Matthew: What was the hardest week at Base Camp for you so far?
Tatum: The loop week, like the 3rd week of the 1st unit. I was completely lost.
Matthew: What was the easiest week at Base Camp for you so far?
Tatum: This week, on HTML. I guess because it's more hands on, rather than looking at the book.
Matthew: What has been your greatest victory while studying at Base Camp?
Tatum: Surviving each unit project. In the first unit, I was not the strongest. The first unit was a struggle. I got in contact with a former student, Tyler Irving. He walked me through the course work.
Matthew: What was your favorite thing to study here at Base Camp?
Tatum: Before Base Camp, I was mainly into the hardware of computers, but I am liking HTML and CSS a lot.
